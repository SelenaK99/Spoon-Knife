SelenaK99 Thoughts This Evening

Derive strength from your otherness.

Instead of trying to deny the parts of yourself that make you feel “othered”, embrace them and find or create spaces that celebrate and positively utilize your otherness.

"As a leader, you don’t take all the risks; you teach your team how to take and manage risk. Mutualize risk and make people feel supported."

- Julie Harris

3 step process for taking clear-eyed risks:

1) Look at a raw idea.
2) Strip away as much uncertainty as possible.
3) Make a decision.

and “lean on your tribe” when taking clear-eyed risks.
